About Bean & Brew Cafe

Live Music: Customers enjoying live band performances at Bean & Brew Cafe.

Game Nights: Friends having fun with board games and great company.

Productive Vibes: Our cafe is a popular spot for working and studying.

Community: Friends spending quality time together at Bean & Brew.

Bean & Brew Cafe is a locally owned coffee shop in Johannesburg, South Africa. We created a warm and welcoming space where people can enjoy high-quality coffee, delicious food, and a relaxed atmosphere. Our café has become a favourite spot for students, professionals, and locals who want more than just a quick cup of coffee. Whether customers are meeting friends, studying, working remotely, or simply taking a break from their busy day, Bean & Brew Café provides the perfect environment to relax and recharge. From live music nights and board game sessions to a comfortable workspace with free Wi-Fi, we aim to be more than just a coffee shop — we want to be a community hub where people connect, create memories, and enjoy meaningful experiences together. We are committed to excellent customer service, affordable prices, and creating a comfortable environment that keeps customers coming back.
